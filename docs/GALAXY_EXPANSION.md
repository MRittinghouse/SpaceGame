## Economy Design
- **Agricultural systems** (Verdant, Haven's Rest): Food surplus, need machinery
- **Mining systems** (Breakstone, Iron Depths): Raw materials, need food and fuel
- **Industrial systems** (Forgeworks): Machinery production, salvage and refining hub
- **Trade hubs** (Nexus Prime, Stellaris Port): Best prices, luxury goods
- **Research systems** (Axiom Labs, Nova Research): High-tech production, advanced refining
- **Frontier** (Crimson Reach): High risk, rare salvage finds

## Trade Routes (suggested)
1. Buy food at Verdant -> Sell at Breakstone
2. Mine ore at Breakstone -> Refine at Forgeworks -> Sell metals at Nexus Prime
3. Salvage at Crimson Reach -> Sell electronics at Axiom Labs
4. Refine crystals at Axiom Labs -> Sell rare metals at Stellaris Port
