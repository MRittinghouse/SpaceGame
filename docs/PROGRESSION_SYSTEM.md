## Skill Trees

### Trading Mastery
1. **Negotiator** (3 levels) - -2% purchase price per level
2. **Market Eye** (1 level) - See detailed price trends (requires Negotiator)
3. **Bulk Trader** (2 levels) - -5% on 10+ unit purchases (requires Negotiator)
4. **Trade Network** (2 levels) - +10% sell price bonus (requires Market Eye)
5. **Market Insider** (1 level) - See remote system prices (requires Trade Network)

### Resource Gathering
1. **Efficient Drills** (3 levels) - -15% drill time per level
2. **Keen Scanner** (3 levels) - +1 scan charge per level (requires Efficient Drills)
3. **Rich Veins** (2 levels) - +25% rare ore chance per level (requires Efficient Drills)
4. **Master Extractor** (3 levels) - -20% extraction time (requires Keen Scanner)
5. **Refining Knowledge** (1 level) - Unlocks advanced recipes (requires Master Extractor)
