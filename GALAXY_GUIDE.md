# Galaxy Guide - The Known Systems

Welcome to the galaxy! Here's your guide to the five major systems in charted space.

---

## 🌟 **Nexus Prime** (Trade Hub)
**Coordinates:** (0, 0)
**Danger Level:** Safe
**Faction:** Nexus Trade Consortium

The galaxy's premier trading hub where all major trade routes converge. A bustling center of commerce and diplomacy.

**Stations:**
- **Central Exchange** - The largest trading station in known space (Docking: 100 CR)
- **Freight Depot** - Bulk cargo and wholesale trading (Docking: 50 CR)

**Economy:**
- **Produces:** Manufactured Goods, Electronics
- **Imports:** Food, Raw Materials, Luxury Goods
- **Tariff:** 2%

**Best for:** Selling manufactured goods and electronics. Central hub for trade routes.

---

## 🌾 **Verdant** (Agricultural)
**Coordinates:** (100, 50)
**Danger Level:** Safe
**Faction:** Verdant Agricultural Cooperative
**Distance from Nexus Prime:** ~112 units

Lush garden worlds covered in endless fields and hydroponic towers. The sector's agricultural heartland.

**Stations:**
- **Agri-Hub One** - Agricultural distribution hub (Docking: 75 CR)

**Economy:**
- **Produces:** Food, Textiles, Organic Goods
- **Imports:** Machinery, Fuel, Manufactured Goods
- **Tariff:** 1%

**Best for:** Buying food cheap! Excellent source of organic goods. Great starting trade route from Nexus Prime.

---

## ⚙️ **Forgeworks** (Industrial)
**Coordinates:** (-80, 60)
**Danger Level:** Moderate
**Faction:** Forgeworks Industrial Syndicate
**Distance from Nexus Prime:** ~100 units

A world of towering factories and orbital shipyards. Smoke and steel define this industrial powerhouse.

**Stations:**
- **Main Foundry** - Massive industrial complex (Docking: 150 CR)
- **Salvage Yards** - Scrap and refurbished parts (Docking: 25 CR)

**Economy:**
- **Produces:** Machinery, Manufactured Goods, Common Metals
- **Imports:** Food, Fuel, Raw Materials
- **Tariff:** 3%

**Best for:** Buying machinery and manufactured goods. Sells food at premium prices.

---

## ⛏️ **Breakstone** (Mining)
**Coordinates:** (50, -100)
**Danger Level:** Dangerous
**Faction:** Independent Miners Guild
**Distance from Nexus Prime:** ~112 units

A lawless frontier system where fortune-seekers brave the asteroid belts for precious ores and rare metals.

**Stations:**
- **Rock Haven** - Rough mining outpost (Docking: 100 CR)

**Economy:**
- **Produces:** Common Metals, Rare Metals, Raw Materials
- **Imports:** Food, Machinery, Fuel
- **Tariff:** 2%

**Best for:** Buying metals cheap. High-risk, high-reward trading. Watch your back!

---

## 🔬 **Axiom Labs** (Research)
**Coordinates:** (-50, -80)
**Danger Level:** Moderate
**Faction:** Axiom Research Institute
**Distance from Nexus Prime:** ~94 units

Gleaming research facilities orbiting pristine worlds. The cutting edge of science and medical advancement.

**Stations:**
- **Research Station Alpha** - State-of-the-art research facility (Docking: 200 CR)

**Economy:**
- **Produces:** Electronics, Medical Supplies, Luxury Goods
- **Imports:** Rare Metals, Food, Raw Materials
- **Tariff:** 4% (highest in the sector!)

**Best for:** Selling rare metals at premium. Buying high-tech medical supplies and electronics.

---

## 💰 Profitable Trade Routes

### **Beginner Routes:**
1. **Verdant → Forgeworks** (~141 units)
   - Buy Food cheap at Verdant
   - Sell Food high at Forgeworks
   - Return with Machinery

2. **Breakstone → Axiom Labs** (~100 units)
   - Buy Rare Metals at Breakstone
   - Sell at premium to Axiom Labs
   - Return with Electronics

### **Advanced Routes:**
3. **Triangle Route:** Verdant → Forgeworks → Breakstone → Nexus Prime
   - Food → Machinery → Metals → Manufactured Goods
   - Requires careful fuel management!
